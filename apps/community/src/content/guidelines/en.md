## OBIGBO Community Guidelines

Welcome to OBIGBO — a digital home for the global Igbo community. These guidelines exist to keep our platform a welcoming, respectful, and safe space for everyone.

## Our Core Values

- **Respect** — Treat every member with dignity and kindness.
- **Authenticity** — Be honest about who you are and your connection to the Igbo community.
- **Inclusion** — Welcome members from all backgrounds, locations, and generations.
- **Cultural pride** — Celebrate and preserve Igbo language, history, and traditions.

## Expected Behaviour

### Be Respectful

Engage with others as you would wish to be engaged. Disagreements are welcome; personal attacks, harassment, or hate speech are not.

### Protect Privacy

Do not share another member's personal information without their consent. What members share in private conversations stays private.

### Share Authentically

Post content you believe to be accurate. Misinformation, spam, and scam attempts will be removed and may result in account suspension.

### Keep It Community-Focused

Content and conversations should contribute positively to the community. Commercial solicitation without prior approval from administrators is not permitted.

## Prohibited Content

- Hate speech, discrimination, or content that demeans any person or group
- Harassment, bullying, or threats of any kind
- Sexual or violent content that is not clearly educational or cultural in context
- Misinformation or deliberate deception
- Spam, unsolicited commercial messages, or pyramid schemes
- Content that violates applicable laws or regulations

## Consequences

Violations of these guidelines may result in content removal, temporary suspension, or permanent ban, depending on severity. Administrators will act in good faith and with consistency.

## Reporting

If you see content that violates these guidelines, please use the report function. All reports are reviewed by our moderation team.

## Changes to These Guidelines

These guidelines may be updated from time to time. Members will be notified of significant changes.

_By continuing as a member of OBIGBO, you agree to abide by these guidelines._
